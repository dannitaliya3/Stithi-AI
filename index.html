The Challenge

Traditional Education is Slow and Insight-Limited

Schools struggle with manual evaluation and lack meaningful academic insights.

1. Time-Consuming Grading

Teachers spend hours checking answer sheets manually instead of focusing on actual teaching.

2. Limited Learning Insights

Schools only see marks, not the real learning patterns behind them. Superficial data prevents action.

3. Hidden Learning Gaps

Students don't identify conceptual weaknesses until exams arrive, missing chances to improve.

4. Inefficient Parent-Teacher Communication

PTMs often lack structured performance data, making conversations less productive.

How It Works

Stithi integrates directly into your existing assessment workflow.

1. Upload Answer Sheets

Teachers upload scanned PDFs or photographed answer sheets directly to the Stithi platform.

2. Document Processing

The system automatically detects pages, segments questions and answers, and prepares the document for AI analysis.

3. Handwriting Recognition

Advanced OCR and computer vision models read handwritten text and convert it into structured digital content.

4. AI Answer Evaluation

Stithi evaluates answers using AI models that analyze correctness, structure, and conceptual understanding.

5. Learning Pattern Analysis

Assessment results are analyzed to identify learning gaps, topic weaknesses, and class performance patterns.

6. Insights & Performance Dashboards

Teachers, students, and parents receive detailed insights through interactive dashboards and performance reports.
